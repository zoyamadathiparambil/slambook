Dreamcatcher

Trapped in isolation,
A Feeling of smother; arise.
The distress and grief collision,
A nightmare in disguise.

Fake friends betraying you,
Solitude skating through the way.
Bliss and euphoria, who?
I Knew only about immense dismay.

There might be no one,
No one to be by your side,
Except, just like a small beam of sun,
A hallucination abides.

Your dreams are like a way to light.
Drift away with them.
Let them control you for the night,
Your fantasy is a gem.

Not a soul can stop you in your illusion,
Like a dreamcatcher, positive visions approach.
Create your characters like a magician,
You are your coach.

Do what you love the most,
Nobody can interrupt you.
The place where you decide your post,
A night journey anew.

~Svetlana bebo
